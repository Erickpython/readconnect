Team

Our team at Read Connect is driven by a shared commitment to improving literacy and education for children in underserved communities. We come together with a unified goal of empowering young learners, knowing that by fostering a love for reading and providing essential resources, we can make a lasting impact on their futures.

Join the Team

At Read Connect, we're looking for passionate and dedicated individuals who are eager to make a lasting impact in their communities.

Our ideal teammate is someone who shares our vision of empowering children through literacy and education. We value people who are innovative, collaborative, and have a genuine commitment to improving the lives of students by fostering a LOVE FOR LEARNING.

If you're driven to lead or implement Read Connect in a different area, we need you to be proactive, organized, and ready to engage with both students and teachers to create a nurturing learning environment. Join us, and together we can fight illiteracy and empower young minds!
