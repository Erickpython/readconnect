Literacy - Language Development

Overview

We empower students with vital reading and comprehension skills. Through engaging activities like interactive read-aloud, play-based learning, and peer tutoring, we foster a love for reading while boosting literacy. By providing quality storybooks and digital resources, we create a rich, supportive learning environment that fuels students' growth and enthusiasm for reading.

Our Efforts and Why It Matters

In resource-limited communities, literacy can hinder personal and academic growth. Our Literacy Program breaks down this barrier by making reading fun and accessible through engaging activities.
By building vocabulary, comprehension, and confidence, we equip students with the skills needed for success in school, lifelong learning, and community impact.
